Hi, my name is Ryan Faulkner I have recently completed my Masters degree at McGill University in Montreal, Quebec where I am a member of the Reasoning and Learning lab. I am currently working as a research analyst for the Wikimedia Foundation in San Francisco CA.

 

Research

 

Currently I am working on the Editor Engagement Experiments team at the Wikimedia Foundation the as a data engineer and research analyst. The focus of my role is to 1) build infrastructure for metrics gathering and data ETL operations (code base in GitHub and documentation) and 2) perform subsequent statistical analyses on the user data - some examples include our experiments on Post-Edit Feedback and Timestamp Modification.

My primary academic research interests lay in Machine Learning, however I have a strong interest in the related fields of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Bayesian Learning. My thesis research involves using deep belief networks to simulate domains for control in reinforcement learning problems. Much of the relevant literature in the area of deep belief networks is fairly new and has been developed in the last five to ten years.
