As of April 2015 I have joined Deepmind as a Research Engineer in London, UK.

I completed my Masters degree at McGill University in Montreal in Fall of 2010, Quebec where I was a member of the Reasoning and Learning lab. I subsequently worked as a Research Analyst for the Wikimedia Foundation (2010-2013) and then as an Analytocs and Data Engineer at Flickr (2013-2015) in San Francisco CA.

 

Research

 

My primary academic research interests lay in Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning. My prior research involved using deep belief networks to simulate domains for control in reinforcement learning problems.
