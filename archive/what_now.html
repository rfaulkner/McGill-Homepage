What now?


For now I've finished up the major part of my Masters research. However, I'm still thinking on where to go next. Specifically, I'd like to figure out the most effective direction to proceed with the goal of demonstrating how agents can benefit by acting in the real world with generative models. My supervisor and I have briefly discussed some options. One area that we've concluded may warrant some interest: using this approach to explore the performance of agents in environments where the state transition probabilities are changing. I've slightly tweaked my RL environment to test such an environment: RL Env. These maps can be dynamically generated by changing the location of the objects (blank squares).
